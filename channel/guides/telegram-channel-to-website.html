Telegram Channel to Website: A Practical Guide

If you publish tours, guides, menus, or catalogs in Telegram, you already have the content. Google does not index your feed like a real site. A separate domain with structured pages fixes that.

1. Audit your channel

Split posts into products (prices, dates), guides (how-to, FAQ), and noise (polls, casual promos). Tag sync-worthy posts with #guide or #tour.

2. Map site structure

Home, product/tour pages, guide articles, about, booking CTA. See our Africa Dream demo for a travel example.

3. Choose bot vs custom site

Instant bots give you a subdomain and a post mirror. Custom builds give brand design, your domain, 95–100 PageSpeed, and filtered sync. We use the same Telethon stack as Barakhlo (38+ channels).

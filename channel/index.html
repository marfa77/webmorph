FAQ

Do I stop posting in Telegram?

No. Telegram stays your daily feed and community. The website catches Google search and gives you a link for ads, email signatures, and partners.

How often does sync run?

Typically once a week — same cron model as Barakhlo. Daily is possible for high-volume channels; we quote separately.

Who is this for?

Cooking & food blogs, health & wellness, auto & tech reviews, travel, finance, education, Telegram-first shops, local services, niche media — anyone with structured posts (recipes, prices, guides, reviews) that deserve permanent URLs.

Is this the same tech as Barakhlo?

Same Telethon parser and regex prefilter as Barakhlo. Difference: Barakhlo serves listings from Postgres + Next.js; Channel → Site stores normalized posts in a content database and publishes fast static HTML to your domain. No LLM in the sync path.

Why is there a monthly fee?

Auto-sync is a service, not a one-time export: we run the parser, keep the content database, rebuild pages, deploy, and monitor. That is the Care plan (~$25–30/mo after the included 3 months). Without it you get a static launch snapshot — fine if you do not need updates.
